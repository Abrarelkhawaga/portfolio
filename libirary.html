Bibliotheca Alexandrina

The Bibliotheca Alexandrina is a modern library and cultural center in Alexandria, Egypt. It serves as a hub for knowledge and cultural activities

The Bibliotheca Alexandrina is one of the most important cultural and scientific landmarks in Egypt and the world. It is located on the Mediterranean coast in Alexandria, near the site of the ancient Library of Alexandria, which was one of the greatest centers of knowledge in history. The modern library was designed to bring back the spirit of learning, research, and cultural exchange that the ancient library represented. The building itself is an architectural masterpiece with a unique circular design that resembles the rising sun, symbolizing the spread of knowledge. Inside, it contains millions of books in different languages, as well as rare manuscripts, digital archives, and specialized libraries for children, young people, and researchers. It also includes museums, art galleries, and planetarium shows. The library is not just a place for reading, but also a major cultural hub that hosts international conferences, exhibitions, workshops, and educational programs. It plays a big role in promoting science, culture, and dialogue between civilizations, making it a symbol of knowledge and enlightenment in modern Egypt.
