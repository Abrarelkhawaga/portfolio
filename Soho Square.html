Soho Square

A glowing world where luxury never sleeps, and every corner pulses with life, music, and modern magic.

SOHO Square is one of the most luxurious and lively entertainment destinations in Sharm El Sheikh. It is a modern lifestyle hub that brings together shopping, dining, entertainment, and nightlife in one beautifully designed place. The square is famous for its dazzling lights, musical fountain shows, ice skating rink, international restaurants, cafes, and high-end shops. Visitors can enjoy live performances, cultural events, and a vibrant atmosphere that continues late into the night. Whether you are looking for relaxation, fine dining, or exciting entertainment, SOHO Square offers a unique experience that blends modern luxury with a lively social vibe in the heart of the Red Sea resorts.
