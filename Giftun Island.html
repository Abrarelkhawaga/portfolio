Giftun Island

An untouched jewel of the Red Sea, where nature breathes in colors you’ve never seen before.

Giftun Island is one of the most beautiful natural islands in the Red Sea, located near Hurghada. It is part of a protected marine reserve known for its crystal-clear waters, white sandy beaches, and vibrant coral reefs. The island is a perfect escape from city life, offering visitors an unforgettable experience surrounded by pure nature. The underwater world around Giftun is rich with colorful fish and coral formations, making it a top destination for snorkeling and diving enthusiasts. Boat trips to the island usually include relaxing on the beach, swimming in calm lagoons, and exploring one of the most pristine environments in Egypt. It is a true paradise where nature remains untouched and breathtaking.
