Sahl Hasheesh

A serene coastal masterpiece where luxury flows as gently as the tide and silence becomes a form of beauty.

Sahl Hasheesh is one of the most luxurious and peaceful destinations on the Red Sea coast near Hurghada. It is famous for its long golden sandy beaches, crystal-clear turquoise waters, and elegant resort-style design that offers a perfect blend of nature and sophistication. The area is designed as a high-end seaside retreat, featuring luxury hotels, private beaches, golf courses, and a stunning promenade overlooking the sea. One of its highlights is the Sunken City, an underwater archaeological-style site that attracts divers from all over the world. With its calm atmosphere, clean environment, and breathtaking views, Sahl Hasheesh is a perfect escape for relaxation, romance, and luxury experiences away from the noise of crowded cities.
